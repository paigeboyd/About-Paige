Paige Boyd

I'm a 19 year old Interactive Multimedia Design
Student from Lurgan, Northern Ireland.

I'm passionate about creating pretty things for the web and I want to be a UI Designer/Developer someday, for now I'm happy to learn new and awesome things to help me get there! I love being involved in every stage of the process, from researching, wireframing and creating mockups to coding and seeing the finished product.

I became interested in website design when I was no age in primary school when one of my teachers introduced me to a website for primary school kids to create their own homepages using a version of very basic HTML and from there I was hooked! I studied ICT right through school and also became interested in art and design, so combining my love for the two seemed like the obvious thing to do. I was lucky enough to get the opportunity to have a week long work placement with a web design studio in Belfast during my first year of A-levels and I fell in love and, as they say, the rest is history! During this placement I also worked closely with the marketing team and have had a growing interest in digital marketing, especially when it comes to using social media and writing blog posts.

Now here I am, 3 years later and I'm still super enthusiastic about all things web. I've also found a new love for illustrating and graphic design and hope to find myself an awesome year-long placement with an awesome web design studio where I can combine my passions, improve my skills, create awesome things and, most importantly, learn from awesome people!
